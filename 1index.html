1.maggie recipe

  • Time: 10min
  • Serves: 3
Ingredients Quantity
maggie packets 2
water 1 glass
masala 1 spoon

Step 1: pour water into a bowl and boil for 2 min on medium flame.

Step2: add maggie into water

Step3: add maggie masala into it and stir for 2 min

Step4:serve it in a plate and your delicious maggie masala is ready to have ...enjoy food!!!!!.....

2.Omilate Recipe

  • Time: 10 min
  • Serve: 2
Ingredients Quantity
eggs 2
oil 1 spoon
salt 1 spoon

Step1:Put a pan on medium flame and pour one spoon of oil in it

Step2:add egg into it and fry it on a high flame

Step3:sprinkle salt on it and serve it on a plate

Step4:spicy omilate is ready to eat

3.Lassy Recipe

  • Time: 5min
  • Serve: 1
Ingredients Quantity
curd 1.cup
water 1 cup
sugar 3 spoons

Step1:mix one cup of curd with one cup of water and beat it hardly

Step2:after mixing well,add 3 spoons of sugar into it

Step3:put in a fridge and let it cool for few minutes

Step4:cool and sweet lassy ready to drink
